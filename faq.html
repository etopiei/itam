FAQ

Q: There's this really great show that should be added to this site, how do I go about getting it added?

A: itam is open source so you can contribute on Github, if you're unsure how to do this you can also just email me and I'll add it as soon as possible, though this method may be slower.


Q: How does itam make money and not collect information on users?

A: Short answer, I don't make money from itam, if you would like to donate there is a button on the homepage to donate via paypal.
